About HematoVision

Learn more about our medical diagnostic platform

Our Mission

HematoVision is dedicated to improving hematological diagnostics through advanced AI technology. Our platform assists medical professionals in quickly and accurately identifying blood cell types.

Our Team

We are a team of medical researchers, data scientists, and software engineers working together to create tools that enhance diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.

Blood Cell Types

Our system classifies four main types of white blood cells: eosinophils, lymphocytes, monocytes, and neutrophils.

High Accuracy

With our deep learning model, we achieve classification accuracy above 99% on test datasets.

Medical Applications

Our technology supports diagnosis of various conditions including infections, leukemia, and immune disorders.
